About Me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Toronto, working in the DGP lab under sepervision of Prof. Karan Singh. I am interested in 3D Modelling, Geometry Processing and Geometric Deep Learning.

I also previoulsy worked at Xtreme  Labs as a mobile app developer, Adobe Research and Conceptualiz Inc. Currently I am working part-time as an R&D engineer at SideFx.
